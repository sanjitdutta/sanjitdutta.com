01 About Me

Sanjit is a Manager within PwC Strategy&'s M&A deals platform where he focuses primarily on due diligence for private equity clients. He is passionate about business transformation through technology, and has a spectrum of strategy, management, and technology consulting experience. Most of Sanjit's sector experience falls within Private Equity, Technology, Entertainment & Media, Telecommunications, and Healthcare. Areas of expertise include:

  • Product Technology and Operations Due Diligence
  • Technology Strategy
  • DevOps & Cloud Architecture
  • Agile Coaching & Software Delivery
Outside of the office, his interests include web development, graphic design, product design, music & instruments, travel, and health. Sanjit graduated with his B.S. in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
