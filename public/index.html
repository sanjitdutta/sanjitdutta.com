About Me

Sanjit is an MBA Candidate at The Wharton School of The University of Pennsylvania with a concentration in Entrepreneurship & Innovation and Finance. He is passionate about product technology and is immersed in the world of technology, startups, and venture capital.

Sanjit was most recently a Manager within PwC Strategy&'s M&A deals platform where he focused primarily on product technology and operational due diligence of technology target companies for private equity clients. He has a spectrum of strategy, management, and technology consulting experience.

Outside of the office/Zoom-room, his interests include web development, graphic design, product design, music & instruments, travel, and health. Sanjit's undergraduate studies include a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
